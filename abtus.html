Formation

Our team formed not because of our shared skills, but also due to a deeply impactful experience of one of our friends. A close friend of ours, passionate about drone shooting, attempted to capture stunning images of the beautiful Guizhou Province, for a coursework using a drone. Unfortunately, an unexpected mist blurred out all the images, ruining the opportunity. This incident made us realize how crucial it is to develop a technology than can be present for people to use and overcome these challenges. Our abilities and shared passion for innovation and preventing the disappointments solidified our dedication to create a specialized software to tackle such problems making our team a perfect fit for this competition.

Our Motivation

While our friends' experience with the mist highlighted the need for a better image restoration technology, our concern for the future motivated us to create this innovation the most. In the future, AI will be increasingly used in dangerous missions, such as rescuing, but we feared that adverse weather could impair and mislead AI’s ability to make accurate decisions, such as detection of victim, or surrounding dangers. This fear inspired us to create a solution that can enhance existing weather degrade image restoration techniques, significantly improve the performance in practice, and reduce the computational costs so the model could function effectively in such scenarios where fast decision has to be made. Our big goal is to make an impact on AI rescuing, saving lives of both rescuers and victims and preventing tragedies caused by AI’s unreliability through our innovation.
