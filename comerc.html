SFTW Market and Business Model

SFTW Market and Strategy

Market

Customer and Target Segments

  • B2B: Industries like autonomous driving, drones, and surveillance systems (e.g., Baidu Apollo, XPeng). Efficiency and reliability are key to their operations.
  • B2C: Personal users of drones, AR/VR devices, and smartphones benefit from SFTW’s ability to restore image clarity in adverse weather.
  • B2G: Governmental organizations, such as firefighters and disaster-management agencies, require tools for real-time image and video restoration in emergencies.

China’s AI policies, such as "十四五"规划, support innovations like SFTW. The autonomous driving market is projected to exceed ¥700 billion by 2030, while the AI surveillance market is valued at ¥1 trillion. Consumer drones are expected to reach ¥60 billion by 2025.

Competition

Competitors: Xiaomi (consumer electronics) and Hikvision (security cameras).

  • SFTW Advantage: Superior image clarity, combined weather-handling capabilities, and practicality for AI models in diverse industries.
  • Challenge: Established competitors require vigorous marketing to highlight SFTW’s unique benefits.

Go-To-Market Strategy

SFTW will launch an online platform/app for individual users and businesses to test its capabilities under various weather conditions. Feedback from these users will refine the model before targeting high-stakes applications like rescue operations. After initial refinement, we will expand to B2B sectors (e.g., security camera manufacturers) and later target B2G sectors through strategic partnerships.

Business Model

  • Costs: Server rentals (~¥1137.6/month) and minimal utility fees due to remote operations.
  • Revenue Model: Basic version (static images): Free. Advanced versions (real-time video): Tiered pricing (e.g., ¥19,999 for B2B).
  • Affordability: Priced competitively against models costing ¥50,000-¥200,000.

Fundraising

Expected Revenue Growth:

  • 2025: 50% growth via B2B/B2C sales.
  • 2026: 80% growth through government rescue partnerships.
  • 2027: Revenue doubles, driven by B2G collaborations.

Sources of Funding:

  • Venture Capital: Jiadao Capital, Yaotu Capital, Sequoia Capital, Shanghai Kechuang.
  • Government Grants: "十四五"规划 and AI subsidies support R&D and commercialization.
  • Crowdfunding: Platforms like Modian (摩点) and JD Crowdfunding.
