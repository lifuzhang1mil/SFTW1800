What is SFTW?

Our model, SFTW (Self-Guided Filter TransWeather), is a solution designed to restore weather-degraded images by removing noise and enhancing clarity in complex and dynamic extreme weather conditions. The advanced LV+SFTW version has even better features including real-time video processing, specifically designed for industries that require live, high-quality video inputs under complex weather scenarios.

Our innovation, SFTW (Self-Guided Filter TransWeather), is currently the most effective restoration model available, specifically designed to overcome adverse weather conditions on input. It uses advanced transformer base, a new-added self-guided-filtering layer, with real-time video processing capabilities, SFTW restores weather-degraded images to pristine clarity under ALL possible extreme conditions.

The impact of SFTW will extend across industries. For autonomous-vehicles, it will ensure reliable navigation by enhancing sensor visibility under challenging weather. For AR/VR devices like Vision Pro, it significantly improves user experience by restoring clarity on the cameras. Drones and surveillance-systems benefit from the extended live-video functionality, while personal users can now enjoy the freedom of aerial-photography, anywhere+everywhere.

BY 2030, the market for autonomous technologies, AV industries, are projected $2.1 trillion. SFTW has massive business potential to become the standard for weather-degraded image restoration. Practical, strong, and revolutionary- SFTW is the future of tools ensuring safety of computer vision technology.
